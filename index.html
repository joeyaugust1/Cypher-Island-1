Cypher Island – Part One

You're on a flight to Germany when the plane suddenly hits massive turbulence. Chaos erupts — the lights flicker, passengers scream, and then darkness. By some twist of fate, the plane crash-lands on an unknown island. When you regain consciousness, you're alone, injured, and disoriented — your health is critical (10%). You must search the crash site for anything useful — clues, supplies, or answers.
