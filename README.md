# Cypher-Island-1
Cypher Island A browser-based survival text adventure game.  Cypher Island is a survival-themed, text-based adventure game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After surviving a mysterious plane crash, players must explore a vast and treacherous island, manage their needs, uncover hidden clues, and make critical decisions in a race against time.
Story Overview

You awaken at the plane crash site, alone and disoriented. With no clear direction, your only option is survival. As you explore Cypher Island, you’ll discover hints about your location and your potential way off the island. Survival means more than food and shelter — it means decoding the island's secrets and finding a path to escape.

Your final goal: reach the end location and escape the island.


---

Core Features

Dynamic Day/Night Cycle: Time progresses with every action. Some events or paths are only available at certain times.

Location-Based Exploration: Start at the Plane Crash Site, then discover new locations like the Beach, Coast Cliffs, and secret areas revealed through clues.

Stat Management: Monitor hunger, fatigue, and health — let any drop too low, and you risk death.

Inventory System: Find items by searching, which can be used or bartered to progress or survive.

Random Travel Events: Encounter enemies, hidden places, or helpful strangers while traveling.

Clue & Task Mechanics: Some paths require specific items or completed tasks before unlocking, encouraging exploration and backtracking.

Expandable World: New locations and interactions become available as players gather clues.
